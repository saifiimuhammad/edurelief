How to start a GoFundMe

Step 1

Our tools help create your fundraiser

Click the ‘Start a GoFundMe’ button to get started. You’ll be guided by prompts to add fundraiser details and set your goal, which can be changed anytime.

Step 2

Share your fundraiser link to reach donors

Once live, share your fundraiser link with friends and family to start gaining momentum. You’ll also find helpful resources for running your fundraiser in your GoFundMe dashboard.

Step 3

Securely receive the funds you raise

Add your bank information, or invite your intended recipient to add theirs, to securely start receiving funds. You don’t need to reach your fundraising goal to start receiving funds.

Biking for Red Cross

Sharon participated in RAGBRAI, an annual cycling event across the state of Iowa. She decided to use her experience to help support a cause she cares about- the American Red Cross! By starting a fundraiser, she raised more than $1,300 to accompany her great accomplishment of riding

Support for healing

When Victor received a diagnosis for ALS, his community wanted to show support. His partner started a GoFundMe to help cover for transportation to treatment, monthly supplements, weekly PT, and much more.

Little League Dreams

The El Segundo Little League team parents started a GoFundMe to help cover the travel, tournament, and family support for their 2023 World Series Tournament. Their community rallied to support the team and their families as they followed the journey to their championship!

Teacher who cares

Mr. Allen is raising money for his students to take hands-on learning trips that can’t be replicated in the classroom. This will allow for many of his students to connect to a learning style they don’t have access to every day. His community is rallying to support the endeavor.
